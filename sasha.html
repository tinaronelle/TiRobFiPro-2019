About My Space

I love my apartment. It is pretty tiny but it is perfect for me any my two children. They share a room and it is plit in half since one is a boy and one is a girl. I live in a building with a lot of young people, so it can get really loud at times. Every space in my apartment is utilized and I wish had just a little more storage. I have a great bathroom. When my friends come over and see it they are jealous that it is so large! I am in show business so a lot of what is hung on the wall are posters and photographs from performances I have done. That and pictures of my kids, there are lots of pictures of them hanging. I would like to collect more art. One day I will get around to that. Purple is my favorite color so my apartment reflects that.
